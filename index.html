Admission Process & Eligibility

Here's a quick overview of the steps to apply for MBBS programs abroad:

Choose Your Country & University

Research universities and select the country that fits your preferences.

Eligibility Check

Ensure you meet the eligibility requirements (age, educational qualifications).

Prepare Documents

Gather necessary documents such as high school transcripts, passport, etc.

Application Submission

Complete the application form for your chosen university.

Wait for Acceptance

Once you receive an acceptance letter, prepare for the next steps.

Visa Application

Apply for a student visa to study in your chosen country.

Secure Accommodation

Arrange for housing either through the university or privately.

Arrival and Orientation

Get ready to start your studies with an orientation to help you settle in.
